## Source locks

### 1. Quanzhen / Dongyuan / Li Ye bridge

Guo Shuchun's Jin-Yuan Mathematics and Quanzhen Taoism is the anchor source for the historical bridge:

- Jin-Yuan mathematicians Li Ye, Zhu Shijie, and Zhao Youqin were heavily influenced by Quanzhen Taoism or were followers.
- Li Ye read Taoist-classic records of circle + nine right-triangle relations called Dongyuan jiurong 洞渊九容.
- Those relations contributed to inscribed-circle/right-triangle study.
- The reasoning directly led to Tianyuan shu, then Two / Three / Four Elements methods.

Interpretation: this is a real historical path from Taoist geometric relation classes to algebraic unknown/element methods.

### 2. Ceyuan Haijing structure

Ceyuan Haijing is not a loose symbolic text. It is a 1248 algebraic-geometry treatise by Li Ye/Zhi with:

- one master diagram: a round town/circle inscribed in a right triangle and square;
- four horizontal lines and four vertical lines;
- a circle center C and named cardinal/intersection points;
- fifteen right triangles generated by the diagram;
- 692 formulae and 170 problems;
- polynomial equations represented through Tianyuan shu.

Key mechanism: many problems are generated from one finite diagrammatic state-space.

### 3. Dongyuan Jiurong / Gougu Rongyuan formula family

Chinese sources describe Gougu Rongyuan as finding a circle diameter from a right-triangle/tangency configuration. The baseline Jiuzhang formula is:

```text
d = 2ab / (a + b + c)
```

where `a,b,c` are the right-triangle legs/hypotenuse.

Song-Yuan/Dongyuan development expands this into multiple circle-containment/tangent relation classes. Sources list a 10-relation family in Ceyuan, with the exact nine Dongyuan relations historically disputed. Later Li Shanlan expands/completes formula tables.

Important pattern:

```text
common numerator: 2ab
variable denominator: admitted contact-position expression in a,b,c
```

Examples from the source summary include denominators of the forms:

```text
a+b+c
 a+b
 a+c
 c
 b+c-a
 a+c-b
 a+b-c
 c-a
 c-b
```

Interpretation: these are not arbitrary formulas. They are relation-class formulas indexed by admitted geometric positions/contact configurations.
