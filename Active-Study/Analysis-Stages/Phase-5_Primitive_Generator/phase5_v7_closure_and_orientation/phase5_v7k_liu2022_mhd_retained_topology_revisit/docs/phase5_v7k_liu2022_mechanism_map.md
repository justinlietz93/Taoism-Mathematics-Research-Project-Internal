# Liu 2022 Mechanism Map

## HFT as boundary/latch separator

The HFT separates high-lying sigmoid/MFR structure from the low-lying filament/sheared-arcade structure. This maps to branch separation and retained-boundary custody in Phase 5.

## Null-point reconnection as endpoint custody transfer

The right footpoint of the high-lying sigmoid moves from P1 to P3. This is a topological connectivity transition, not a mere image displacement.

## Tether-cutting as branch separation

The tether-cutting reconnection cuts the low filament from the high sigmoid. The high branch erupts; the low branch remains stable.

## Synthetic AIA as projection

AIA projections are line-of-sight integrations from 3D plasma state. Projection is downstream from retained state.
