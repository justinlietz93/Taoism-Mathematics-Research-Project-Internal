## A. `The Shape of Understanding`

### Mechanism now locked

The article explicitly builds a ladder:

```text

→ Taiji
→ Yin/Yang
→ binary tree
→ Si-Xiang / 4 appearances
→ Chinese medicine 6 levels
→ Ba-Gua / 8 trigrams
→ I-Ching / 64 hexagrams
→ 10,000 things / infinity
```

### Key exact mechanics

1. Wuji is framed as formless absence / void / unlimited potential.
2. The pattern of Tao is described as an infinite binary tree rising out of Wuji.
3. Si-Xiang is made by taking one Yin/Yang pair and adding another Yin/Yang pair to each branch.
4. The 6-level Chinese medicine view retains the 4 appearances **plus** the Yin/Yang pair that builds the structure.
5. Ba-Gua is made by continuing the same process onto the 4 appearances.
6. Post-Heaven is created by changing the middle line of Heaven and Earth to the opposite; Heaven becomes Fire, Earth becomes Water, and center Earth splits into Earth/Mountain.
7. I-Ching is explicitly an 8×8 trigram product space giving 64 hexagrams.
8. Hexagram dynamics include ordering effects: Heaven-over-Earth and Earth-over-Heaven have different dynamic meanings.

Very strong.

Correct comparison:

```text

  4 equatorial quarter-turn phase seats
  + 2 axial/radix-pole seats
```

This is not proof of identity, but it is a high-grade structural lead.

### B relevance

The article supplies a branch-refinement ladder but not a Fibonacci/Farey recurrence. Do not force the depth-9 B anchor onto it unless a separate source connects the 9-layer/floor behavior.

### L relevance

Strong.


## K. Yin-Yang grid / axis-free overset family

### Core mechanism

The Kageyama/Sato Yin-Yang grid is an overset spherical grid made from two identical component grids combined complementarily with overlap. Each component is a low-latitude patch of a latitude-longitude grid. The Yang coordinate axis is perpendicular to the Yin coordinate axis. The method avoids pole singularity and grid convergence problems by using overlapping re-charts.

### Exact relevance

This is a concrete numerical-method instance of L:

```text
single chart fails at poles
→ decompose sphere into complementary patches
→ patches overlap at boundaries
→ transform/interpolate across boundary
→ continue computation without pole singularity
```

### Code link

`yinyang_transform.py` implements the corresponding coordinate transform:

```text
(x_e,y_e,z_e)=(-x_n,z_n,y_n)
```

The transform is an involutive orientation-preserving 3D re-chart:

```text
M^2 = I
det M = 1
```

### Status

Very strong L external lead. Not Taoist ancient math, but mathematically exact and deliberately named Yin-Yang because of complementarity.
