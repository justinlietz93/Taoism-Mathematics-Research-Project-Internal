## Batch 1: Tao/Yijing structure

### Source: `Tao-Research/out/1_ The Shape of Understanding - by Vincent John.pdf`

**Core claim**: Taoist understanding is presented as a layered generative binary structure: Wuji, Taiji, Yin/Yang, binary tree, 4 appearances, 6 levels, Bagua, I-Ching, 10,000 things.

**Key mechanics read from text**:

- Wuji is described as stillness/formlessness, void/nothingness, preliminary state, absence with potential.
- Taiji is described as the split/line/moment between Yin and Yang.
- The text explicitly states the Tao pattern as an infinite binary tree rising from Wuji.
- Si-Xiang / 4 appearances arise by taking one Yin/Yang pair and adding a Yin/Yang pair to each branch.
- The 6-level view looks at the same structure more finely: the 4 appearances plus the Yin/Yang pair that builds the structure.
- Bagua arises by continuing the same process and applying the pattern to the 4 appearances, producing 8 trigrams.
- Pre-Heaven arrangement is a circular/taiji-like arrangement with observer centered and trigrams understood from the middle looking outward.
- Five-element reading is not five equal outer phases: one Yin/Yang pair is treated as a unified center, leaving 4 lines/processes around it.
- Post-Heaven arrangement is generated by changing the middle line of Heaven/Earth to its opposite, changing visible trigram identity and splitting center Earth into Earth/Mountain.
- I-Ching is 8x8 trigram grid, generating 64 hexagrams, each with top/bottom trigram.

**Q relevance**:

Strong. The document gives:

```text
4 visible appearances
+ 2 builder states
= 6-level carrier reading
```

This aligns structurally with:

```text
4 equatorial Q phases
+ 2 axial/radix-pole seats
= 6-seat carrier
```

**B relevance**:

Medium-to-strong as branch refinement. The document’s binary tree and repeated addition of Yin/Yang pairs is a branch-depth generator. It does not yet show a numeric Fibonacci/Farey floor anchor, but it clearly contains recursive refinement.

**L relevance**:

Strong. Pre-Heaven → Post-Heaven uses internal line inversion and center splitting to produce a re-articulated visible arrangement. That is not merely metaphorical; it is an operator-like transformation of a carried line-state.

**Open questions**:

- Does the article’s “9/21” placement have structural significance beyond page pagination? Do not overclaim. Compare with actual diagram levels, not page number alone.
- Does any Yijing source give a depth-9 or floor-anchor analogue independent of this article?

## Batch 2: Dao De Ching mathematical coordinate papers

### Source: `Tao-Research/out/5d56507fb71b0.pdf`

**Title guess**: `The Myth and Math of Dao De Ching (DDC): The Mathematical Functional Analysis`.

**Core claim**: DDC concepts can be interpreted through functional analysis, Cartesian coordinates, one/two/three-dimensional systems, polar schemes, and dynamic relationships.

**Key mechanics read from text**:

- Functions express motion/change rather than only static equations.
- Dao/De, Yin/Yang, Wu/You, heaven/earth/human, nature are mapped into coordinate systems.
- Dao can be treated as origin/center/core, with others turning around it.
- Wu/You is mapped as non-being/being with negative/positive axis behavior.
- 3D coordinate system uses orthogonal axes for Dao/nature, Yin/Yang, heaven/earth/time/space, Qi/De/all things/humans.
- Polar-coordinate section maps Dao as reference point, De as radius, DDC/Ching as circle, with things following circular paths whose size/shape changes.
- Five elements are mapped into quadrants plus a pole/origin.

**Q relevance**:

Strong external lead for radix/radius/circle/phase mechanics. Dao as pole/reference point and De as radius is exactly the kind of center + circular motion structure that belongs in the Q-reading search.

**B relevance**:

Weak-to-medium. It talks about functions, dependencies, balance, equilibrium, and mediating variables, but not a specific Farey/Fibonacci refinement.

**L relevance**:

Medium. Dimensional escalation 1D→2D→3D→polar and projection onto axes are relevant to lift/re-chart mechanics.

**Caution**:

Mathematical rigor is loose. Use as an external lead, not a proof source.

## Batch 3: Complex algebra / orthogonal legalization

### Source: `Tao-Research/out/The Tao of Math.pdf`

**Core claim as relevant here**: imaginary numbers enter when same-axis real-number representation cannot handle square roots of negatives; they become meaningful on a perpendicular axis, forming the complex plane.

**Key mechanics read from text**:

- Negative numbers have no square roots on the real number line.
- Mathematicians proceeded by treating the square root of a negative as a number and found it solved equations.
- Imaginary numbers have no place on the real number line.
- Imaginary numbers fit on a perpendicular axis.
- Real horizontal axis + imaginary vertical axis fills the complex plane with complex numbers.

**Q relevance**:

Strong for complex axial pair / phase rotation. `i` is not a zero; it is an orthogonal unit.

**B relevance**:

None directly.

**L relevance**:

Exact mechanism-level hit: blocked real-axis operation becomes valid when an orthogonal axis is introduced. This is the dimensional legalization / orthogonal re-articulation mechanism.

**Locked wording**:

Do not call this “almost” the user’s mechanism. This is the same mechanism at the operator level:

```text
blocked same-axis operation
→ orthogonal axis introduced
→ higher-dimensional carrier opens
→ formerly illegal operation becomes legal
→ output becomes phase/rotation rather than collapse
```
